Sentiment analysis is a powerful tool for understanding public opinion, customer feedback, and market trends. With **MrScraper's General Scraper**, you can easily extract user reviews from websites and analyze their sentiment to gain valuable insights.

## Scenario

Imagine you are a market researcher looking to analyze user reviews for a recently released movie on Rotten Tomatoes. You want to gather user reviews to determine the overall sentiment and identify common themes in the feedback.

## Step 1: Create a New AI Scraper

1. Go to the **Scrapers** page by clicking the **triangle icon** on the left sidebar.
2. Click on the **Create Scraper** button.
3. Select the **General Scraper** option.
4. In the **Target URL** field, enter the URL of the Rotten Tomatoes' movie review page you want to scrape. For example: `https://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/predator_badlands/reviews?type=user`.
5. Click on the **Start Scraping** button to start scraping.
