Real estate professionals, investors, and data analysts can leverage MrScraper to collect property listings from public real estate websites.  
Instead of manually browsing through hundreds of listings, you can use the **AI Scraper** to extract property details automatically — with just a URL and a prompt.

Let's walk through a **real-world example** where a real estate investor uses MrScraper to collect data from a live property listing site.


## Scenario

Imagine you're considering buying property in Michigan and want to analyze the market across different cities.  
You want to **compare property prices, sizes, and features** across Michigan to find the best value for your budget.  
Instead of checking multiple listings sites daily, you'll use MrScraper to extract property data from [Redfin](https://www.redfin.com), one of the largest real estate platforms.

## Step 1: Create a New AI Scraper

1. Go to the **Scrapers** page by clicking the **triangle icon** on the left sidebar.
2. Click on the **Create Scraper** button.
3. Select the **Listing Scraper** option.
4. In the **URL** field, enter the URL of the Michigan property listings page: `https://www.redfin.com/state/Michigan`.
5. Click on the **Start Scraping** button to start scraping.
